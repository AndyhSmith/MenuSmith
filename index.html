iPad or iPhone

  1. Launch "Safari" app. This does not work in the "Chrome" app.
  2. At the bottom of your web browser tap the share button.
  3. Scroll down and tap on 'Add to Home Screen'.
  4. You should see a preview of the app. Select add in the top right corner.
  5. Safari will close automatically and you should see the new web app.

Android

  1. Tap the menu icon (3 dots in upper right-hand corner) and tap Add to homescreen.
