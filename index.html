Recipes, Pantry, and Shopping List



Recipes

'Recipes' lists all the recipes you can make with ingredients from your pantry.



The 'all' button lets you explore new recipes with ingredients missing from your pantry.

You can click on any recipe name to find it's preparation instructions.



A recipe will list ingredients, instructions, and more.

Tip: When cooking if you use up an ingredient, click that ingredient twice and it will be added to your shopping list.



Pantry

This is your pantry. Keep it updated with all the food you have in your house to get meal suggestions.



Shopping List

Easily manage your shopping list.

After shopping easily add everything to your pantry.



Privacy

All your data is stored locally on your device.

This protects your privacy but also means you need to do all you pantry managment on one device.
